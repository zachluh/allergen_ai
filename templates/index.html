Anyone should get to eat good food

Created by a college student with allergies to dairy, eggs, nuts and kiwi, Allergen AI aims to help people with restrictive food allergies find options when it comes to eating food that one can actually enjoy. Whether you're a person with restrictive food allergies yourself, a parent that wishes to give their child more possibilities at the dinner table, or just a loved one that cares about another that often has to eat alone, this tool was made for you.
